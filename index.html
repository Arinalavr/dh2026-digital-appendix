Project

Project In Brief

This project explores how different publics perceive an abandoned coastal hotel in Montenegro and uses small-data qualitative methods, 3D/360° documentation, and future AR-based speculative scenarios to test socially acceptable futures for the site.

Site

An abandoned coastal hotel near Petrovac, Montenegro.

Focus

Public perceptions, affective responses, future imaginaries, and possible small-scale reuse.

Methods

Short on-site interviews, thematic coding, photo-elicitation, 360° documentation, and future 3D/AR testing.

Current Stage

Stage 1 short interviews have been analysed. Deep interviews and spatial documentation are ongoing.

Method

AI-Assisted Qualitative Workflow

In this project, I use AI-assisted tools as part of a small-data urban humanities workflow. They help with transcription, candidate coding, synthesis scaffolding, and visual pattern checking. The interpretation stays researcher-led and researcher-verified.

The coding is organized through an Affective / Behavioral / Cognitive framework: AFF for emotions and atmospheres, BEH for practices and actions, and COG for interpretations, knowledge, and imagined futures (Konduri & Lee, 2023).

AI is part of the method: visible, checkable, and useful for small qualitative data.

Stage 1

What The First Stage Interviews Made Visible

Stage 1 findings are preliminary and based on 20 short on-site interviews, researcher-verified coding, visual pattern checks, and close reading of coded excerpts.

Nature anchors the site

Respondents are primarily drawn by the sea, beach, views, and landscape.

The hotel is affectively charged

It is rarely neutral. People respond with sadness, shame, fascination, curiosity, or aesthetic interest.

Context changes interpretation

For respondents who know the history, the hotel also signifies governance failure and wasted public potential.

Non-entry is meaningful

Many users experience the building from outside through safety concerns, indifference, uncertainty, or prior attitudes toward ruins.

Future imaginaries are small-scale and mixed

Respondents tend to imagine combinations of public, creative, recreational, and light commercial uses.

Visual Checks

Python Visual Checks
